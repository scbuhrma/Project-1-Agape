About Us

High up in the Appalachians sits a beautiful bed and breakfast know as The Lodge. Located in Historic West Asheville, The Lodge puts its customers into the heart and soul and Asheville's art scene. Within a 10 minute walk, you can be at some of the best bars and restaurants Asheville has to offer! The Lodge is also on the city bus route which can have you downtown in under 20 minutes. Traveling by bus is highly recommended so that you can experience the true colors Asheville has to offer. We look forward to seeing you at The Lodge!
